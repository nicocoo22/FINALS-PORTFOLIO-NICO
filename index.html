About Me

I'm an IT student at MIT College of Science and Technology, working my way toward a career as a senior web developer. My focus right now is on building solid fundamentals across the front and back end — from HTML, CSS, and JavaScript through to Java, C++, and Python — so I can move comfortably between how a site looks and how it actually works.

I like projects that force me to finish what I start: shipping something small and working beats a big idea that never leaves the drafting table. Outside of coursework, I keep sharpening my Git workflow and design sense in Figma, and I try to bring the same discipline to my faith as I do to my code.
